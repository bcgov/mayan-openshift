Welcome to Mayan EDMS!
======================

.. image:: /_static/mayan_logo.png
  :alt: Logo is a trademark of Roberto Rosario
  :align: center


Mayan EDMS is the most advanced `Free Open Source`_
`Electronic Document Management System`_
available. It is coded in the Python language using the Django_ web
application framework and released under the `GPL 2.0 License`_.

It is also very easy to install and get started:

- :ref:`docker_compose_install`

For additional premium articles, tutorials, and troubleshooting,
join the Knowledge Base community. By joining, you receive access to
hundreds of articles and help support the project. Join today to unlock tons
of exclusive content and contribute to the growth of Mayan EDMS!
